We give the best Services

We provide soft facilities management (FM) services to clients in all major sectors across Australia & New Zealand. By ensuring a safe, compliant and professional built environment, we allow our clients to focus on their core business whilst ensuring a positive experience for their customers and end-users. Soft FM services involve a range of services that maintain the hygiene and security and aesthetic quality of an asset. It may also include the provision of food services.

Through the integration of people, processes, system and technologies, AP Technical's Soft FM services are tailored to meet the unique requirements of each client, and cover the full range of soft services

Soft FM services can be delivered as part of a bundled services contract, combined with Hard FM in an integrated FM contract, or as part of a public private partnership (PPP.) We can also deliver cleaning services directly through our dedicated cleaning company.

Service Overview

Through the integration of people, processes, system and technologies, Ventia Soft FM sevices are tailored to meet the unique requirements of each client, and cover the full range of soft service including :

  • Cleaning
  • Catering
  • Security
  • Pest Control
  • Garden and ground maintainance
  • Waste management
  • Concierge and mailroom
  • Washroom management
  • Window cleaning
  • Utilities
  • Help desk operations

Service Quality

Our Soft FM services put health and safety first, which means we actively manage risk and keep compliance front of mind. From the services we deliver to the way we support our teams, our zero-harm mindset guides everything we do.  

This mindset extends to the planet too, keeping us focused on energy conservation, sustainable waste management and emissions reduction.
